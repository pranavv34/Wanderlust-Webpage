# WANDERLUST - THE TOURIST GUIDE OF THE CITY HYDERABAD
Wanderlust is a website designed to provide information and guidance to tourists who are visiting the city Hyderabad. The goal of this tourist guide project is to help visitors make the most of their trip by providing them with accurate and relevant information about the city Hyderabad.

<h2>PROJECT DETAILS</h2>

1. **Places to Visit:**
   - The guide categorizes popular tourist spots into different sections such as historical monuments, cultural attractions, parks and gardens, shopping areas, and entertainment hubs. Each category provides an extensive list of must-visit places with descriptions, timings, entry fees, and nearby attractions.

2. **How to Reach Hyderabad:**
   - This section provides detailed information on various modes of transportation to reach Hyderabad, including air, rail, and road options. It includes major airports, railway stations, bus terminals, and routes, making it easier for travelers to plan their journey.

3. **Iconic Dishes:**
   - Hyderabad is renowned for its rich culinary heritage. The guide features a curated list of iconic dishes such as Hyderabadi Biryani, Haleem, and Qubani Ka Meetha. Each dish is accompanied by a brief history, ingredients, and recommended places to savor these local delicacies.

4. **Climate:**
   - Understanding the climate is crucial for planning a trip. This section provides information on Hyderabad's weather patterns throughout the year, including temperature ranges, monsoon seasons, and the best time to visit.

5. **Luxurious Hotels:**
   - For those seeking a luxurious stay, the guide lists the top high-end hotels in Hyderabad. It includes details on amenities, services, location, and pricing, helping travelers choose the perfect accommodation for a comfortable and lavish stay.

6. **Local Commute:**
   - Navigating the city is made easier with information on local commute options. This includes details on public transport systems like buses, metro, and auto-rickshaws, as well as car rental services and tips for hassle-free commuting within the city.

7. **History:**
   - Dive into the rich history of Hyderabad with this section, which covers the city's founding, its evolution through various dynasties, significant historical events, and the cultural heritage that shapes its identity today.

8. **Travel Blogs:**
   - Gain insights from seasoned travelers through a collection of travel blogs. These blogs offer personal experiences, travel tips, hidden gems, and recommendations that provide a unique perspective on exploring Hyderabad.
